Access Instructions

Create A Google Account

If you use Gmail, you already have a Google account. Otherwise, you can create a new account here. By default this will create a new gmail.com email address for you, but this is not a requirement. There is an option to register with a pre-existing email address.

Request Access to Google Drive and Calendar

If the shortcuts above send you to an access denied error, email Peter and Zeke your Google account name (email address). Once your account has been granted access, the shortcuts above will take you to the Google Drive and Calendar.
