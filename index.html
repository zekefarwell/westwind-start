Google Account

If you use Gmail, you already have a Google account. Use the same credentials you would for Gmail when prompted to sign in to Google Drive or Calendar.

If you don't use Gmail, you'll need to create a Google account. By default this will create a new gmail.com email address for you, but you can use your current email address instead if you like.

West Wind Google Drive and Calendar Access

Once you have a Google account, it needs to be granted access to our shared Google Drive and Calendar. Many family members' accounts have already been granted access. Try the shortcuts above. After signing in, if you see the West Wind Google Drive and Calendar then your account has access and you are all set. If you see an access denied error, then send Peter, Reilly, and Zeke your Google account email address so we can grant access.
